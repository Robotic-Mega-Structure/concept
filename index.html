Infrastructure refers to the underlying framework or characteristics of a system or organization. The term infrastructure technically only applies to fundamental facilities and systems that serve a country, city or area, such as transportation and communication systems, power plants, schools, and now, thanks to the Internet and online shooping, wearehouses have become one of the most important elements of the infrastructure of the 21st century

While online shopping continues to multiply, part of the system to support growth, it has not optimally developed. One of the problems is forcing the adaptation of the new demand to a pre-existing concept.
Today, automated warehouse are build inside existing structures or pre existing concepts of what a wearehouse should look like, the automated systems are a very basic attempts to reduce human tasks, while improving productivity, efficiency and trying to reduce operating costs that can be real after a large investment and years of operations.
